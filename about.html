About Us

We follow an integrated curriculum that combines both Montessori and Kindergarten approaches.

We offer Playgroup, Nursery, LKG, UKG, and Daycare services.

Why Choose Us

  • Engaging Play Activities
  • Focus on Safety & Hygiene

Our Vision

At Rainbow Roots, our vision is to nurture young minds with love, creativity, and a strong foundation for lifelong learning. We believe in developing holistic skills that support academic, emotional, and social growth.

  • Creative and fun-based teaching methods
  • Strong communication with parents
  • Friendly and nurturing environment
